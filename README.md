# 🩺 Health Digital Twin AI

## 📌 Overview

Health Digital Twin AI is an AI-powered healthcare analytics platform developed using Python and Streamlit. The system creates a digital representation of a patient's health profile and provides intelligent insights through health risk analysis, disease prediction, diet recommendations, health grading, and trend monitoring.

The platform helps users understand their health condition by analyzing important health parameters such as BMI, blood pressure, blood sugar, cholesterol levels, heart rate, and overall health score. It combines traditional health assessment techniques with Machine Learning to provide personalized health intelligence.

## 🏗️ System Architecture

1. Patient enters health information.
2. System calculates BMI and Health Score.
3. Risk analysis modules evaluate diabetes and heart disease risk.
4. Machine Learning model predicts disease probability.
5. Diet Recommendation Engine generates personalized suggestions.
6. Health Grade System assigns an overall health grade.
7. Results are visualized through charts and analytics dashboards.
8. Patient history is stored for future trend analysis.
9. PDF reports are generated for download and record keeping.
